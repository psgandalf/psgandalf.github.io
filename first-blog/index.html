Learning by doing

Micke MAY 30, 2021

Once upon a time there where one computer magazine Byte Magazine. All other where nothing in compare. I had a local store ordering in one copy for me and I had to go there once a month to collect it. The best part of the reading was Chaos Manor. What it taught me was that there will be problems when you use computers but if you put your mind to it you will solve it.

Learning by doing

So why not do a chaos manor? Instead of giving the final solution to the problems, why not showing the pains it took to get it up running and what I had to do to make it work?

What kind of stuff will it cover?

First of all I still am a computer nerd. So the main part will of course be computer related. I like programming but to do that you often have to fix something not working. But I tend to stumble into walls in other topics so who knows where we will go.....
